Inside In Market Strategy for NIFTY-50 stocks

This script scans NIFTY-50 stocks using Zerodha’s 1-minute candle stick data. It validates data integrity and applies a sequential candle-engulfing filter at 09:30, 09:45, and 10:00. Stocks passing each stage move forward, progressively narrowing selection based on early-market volatility expansion using precise pandas time-series analysis.

(PS: It will not show the data for holidays(i.e Saturday/Sunday/NSE holidays))
